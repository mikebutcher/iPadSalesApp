Guaranteed Scale-Free System

Chardon warrants its research developed chemicals and services to be above industry norms and, when used as directed, to be able to produce the desired results, scale-free tubes in cooling, heating and process systems. Provided that Chardon is permitted to control the application of chemicals with proper equipment and controls, to use its service force to monitor applications of its products, and to inspect the applicable system(s) in order to certify that the system(s) are in a clean condition before service begins; and provided that all equipment is allowed to operate as intended, Chardon will unconditionally warrant that our products and services will yield a scale-free system. In the unlikely event any warranted system is scaled after inspection. Chardon will, at its own expense, chemically and/or mechanically clean the system, and provide a report to the system manager regarding the probable reason or reasons why the system was scaled.
